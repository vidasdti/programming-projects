Hiva Restaurant

Hiva Restaurant is one of the famous and popular restaurants in Kerman, known for its high-quality food and pleasant atmosphere. This restaurant is especially recommended for people looking for traditional Iranian dishes with unique flavors. Address: Kerman, Keshavarz Boulevard, between Keshavarz 16 and Keshavarz 18

Pars Hotel Restaurant

Pars Hotel Restaurant in Kerman is one of the luxurious and famous restaurants in the city, located inside Pars Hotel. This restaurant is well known for its elegant atmosphere, excellent service, and diverse menu. Address: Kerman, Jomhouri Eslami Boulevard, Moradi Kermani Boulevard, Moradi Kermani 13, Pars Hotel Kerman

Keykhosro Restaurant

Keykhosro Restaurant is another crowded and popular restaurant in Kerman that serves customers in its outdoor area. This restaurant is famous for its Iranian cuisine, especially its unique kebabs. Address: Kerman, 22 Bahman Boulevard, Shohada Square

Asiab Badi Restaurant

Asiab Badi Restaurant is one of the popular and famous restaurants in Kerman, recognized for its unique atmosphere. With its traditional architectural style and cozy environment, this restaurant provides a special experience for guests. Address: Kerman Province, Kerman County, Mahan District, Haft Bagh Highway
